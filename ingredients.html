Almond Sweet Recipe

This is the recipe of my black coffee

Ingredients(2)

  • 50g coffee beans
  • 250ml water

Don't forget your cup of coffee

Steps(1 to 5)

  1. Grind coffee beans using a grinder
  2. Fill your coffee machine with water
  3. Put a filter for the coffee bean remainings in the coffee machine
  4. Close the lid and Start the coffee machine
  5. Wait until the coffee machine is done making coffee
